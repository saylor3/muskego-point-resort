Why Muskego Point

World-Class Fishing

Lake Vermilion — 40,000 acres and 365 islands — is one of Minnesota's premier muskie and walleye lakes. Muskies up to 55 inches, walleyes past 30.

Private & Unhurried

Each cabin has its own dock on the lake. No crowds, no schedule — just pines, water, and the quiet you drove up here to find.

A Century of History

Originally called Goodwill's, the resort has welcomed families to Lake Vermilion for over 100 years. Some things don't change — and shouldn't.

Built for Families

Cabins sleep 4 to 10 with full kitchens, wood-burning fireplaces, screened porches, and Weber grills. Bring the whole crew.

The Cabins

Eleven cabins, one lake

From a cozy two-bedroom for a couple to a five-bedroom cottage for the whole family reunion. Every cabin has a dock, a fireplace, and a lake view.
