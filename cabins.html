Before You Book

Rental Policies

Rates

Based on 2 guests. $100 per person per week for additional guests. Children under 3 stay free. Daily rate = 1/5 of the weekly rate.

Season & Check-In

May through October. Summer weeks run Saturday to Saturday. Check-in after 4 pm, check-out before 9 am.

Deposit & Cancellation

$500 deposit per cabin per week. Fully refundable if cancelled 90 or more days before your start date.

Spring & Fall Discount

15% lodging discount before Memorial Day and after Labor Day. Two-night minimum. Call to arrange.

Payment

A 3.5% cash discount is built into our pricing. Credit card payments do not receive the discount.

Pets & Tax

We're sorry — no pets. All rentals are subject to applicable Minnesota lodging tax.
