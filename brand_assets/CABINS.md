## Rental policies (from live site)
- Rates based on 2 people; $100/person/week for each additional guest. Children under 3 free.
- Daily rate = 1/5 of weekly rate. No pets. Rentals subject to tax.
- $500 deposit per cabin per week; refundable if cancelled ≥90 days before start.
- 3.5% cash discount built into pricing (credit-card payments don't get the discount).
- **Summer:** weeks run Saturday–Saturday, check-in after 4pm, check-out before 9am.
- **Spring/Fall** (before Memorial Day / after Labor Day): 15% lodging discount, 2-night minimum.
